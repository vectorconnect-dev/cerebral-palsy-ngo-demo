About Our Mission

BrightSteps Foundation is dedicated to improving the lives of children living with cerebral palsy. Through specialized therapy programs, inclusive education initiatives, and family support networks, we help children reach their full potential.

Our mission is to create a world where every child, regardless of physical challenges, has access to opportunity, dignity, and independence.

Our Programs

Therapy Support

Providing physical, occupational, and speech therapy programs tailored to children with cerebral palsy.

Inclusive Education

Partnering with schools to create accessible and inclusive classrooms for children with disabilities.

Community Programs

Engaging volunteers and communities to support families and raise awareness.
