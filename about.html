About BrightSteps

Our mission is to empower children living with cerebral palsy.

Who We Are

BrightSteps Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting children with cerebral palsy through therapy, inclusive education, and community programs.

We believe every child deserves access to opportunity, care, and independence regardless of physical challenges.
